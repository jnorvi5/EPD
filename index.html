Section 2
EPD Adoption Challenge

Why EPD Growth Has Been Slower Than It Should Be

EPD International has created the gold standard for environmental verification. Yet structural barriers on both the supply and demand side limit how quickly the market can adopt EPDs.

EPD International has created the gold standard for environmental verification. But despite the quality and rigor of EPDs, adoption faces barriers:

SUPPLY-SIDE BARRIERS:

  • Small/medium manufacturers find EPD process complex and expensive
  • Many don't understand EPD value proposition (ROI unclear)
  • Limited resources for LCA data collection and application
  • No clear path from “EPD certified” to “increased sales”

DEMAND-SIDE BARRIERS:

  • Architects want sustainable materials but research is fragmented
  • EPD data exists but isn't accessible at point of material selection
  • Comparing EPDs across products requires manual work
  • No infrastructure connects EPD verification to procurement workflow

THE RESULT:

  • → EPD adoption slower than environmental urgency demands
  • → Leading manufacturers get EPDs; small innovators can't compete
  • → EPD investment ROI unclear to prospective applicants
  • → Massive potential impact unrealized

This is the problem GreenChainz was created to solve.

Section 3
Solution – Three Pillars

The GreenChainz Solution – Three Pillars

An integrated platform that supports EPD adoption end-to-end: from certification readiness to data distribution and evidence of market impact.

Intro: An Integrated Platform to Accelerate the EPD Ecosystem

Pillar 1
EPD Adoption Infrastructure
Problem: Small manufacturers can't access EPD certification
Solution: Simplified EPD acquisition support services
What We Do
  • EPD Readiness Assessment
  • Guided EPD Application Support
  • LCA consultant coordination
  • Application project management
  • Programme Operator liaison
Impact for EPD International:
  • ✓ More EPD applications (market expansion)
  • ✓ Higher quality submissions (we pre-validate data)
  • ✓ Access to underserved manufacturer segments
  • ✓ US market penetration (our initial focus)
Pillar 2
EPD Data Distribution
Problem: EPD-verified products aren't reaching buyers effectively
Solution: B2B marketplace connecting verified products to buyers
Role in the partnership
  • Expose EPD International-verified products in a curated marketplace
  • Make EPD data accessible at the point of specification
  • Streamline comparison between products within the same category
  • Create direct line-of-sight from EPD registration to sales opportunities
Impact for EPD International:
  • ✓ Stronger value proposition to manufacturers (“EPD that actually drives demand”)
  • ✓ Increased visibility of the EPD system among architects and procurement teams
  • ✓ Feedback loop on categories and geographies with highest marketplace pull
Pillar 3
EPD Impact Intelligence
Problem: Limited evidence that EPDs shift procurement in practice
Solution: Research and analytics proving that EPDs drive decisions
What We Measure
  • How often EPDs are used in product shortlist decisions
  • Conversion uplift for EPD-certified products vs. non-EPD peers
  • Which EPD attributes matter most to specifiers by segment
  • Case studies connecting EPD rigor to project outcomes
Impact for EPD International:
  • ✓ Evidence base for policy, marketing, and ecosystem engagement
  • ✓ Stories and data to communicate EPD value to manufacturers
  • ✓ Clear link between EPD investment and commercial outcomes
Together, the three pillars form a closed-loop system: manufacturers are supported into EPDs → their products are surfaced where buyers choose → the impact of EPDs on real projects is measured and communicated back into the ecosystem.
